16th Quadrennial General Conference (QGC)

The Association of African Universities (AAU) hosts its Quadrennial General Conference to unite leaders in higher education.

The upcoming 16th Conference will focus on Shaping the Future of Higher Education for Innovation and Sustainable Development of Africa.

Join us for crucial discussions on addressing educational challenges and fostering innovation across the continent. Be part of Africa's educational transformation!

Where

Rabat, Morroco

When

July 7-11, 2025
